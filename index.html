Bill Mead

Web design wizard and educator extraordinaire, he has been shaping the minds of tomorrow's digital creators for over two decades. When he's not busy teaching at UC Davis or wrangling a classroom of eager students, you might find him elbow-deep in HTML or cracking the latest JavaScript puzzle.

With a background as eclectic as his taste in design, Bill's career has spanned everything from llama wrangling (okay, maybe not real llamas) to leading WordCamp Sacramento. If there's one thing he loves more than a clean desk, it's having a good laugh while making the web a better place.
